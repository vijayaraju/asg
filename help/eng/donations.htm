How may users send a donation?

For the donation we have chosen PayPal , one of the most important online payment solution.
PayPal in an eBay company, founded in 1998.

It enables any individual or business with an email address to securely, easily and quickly send and receive payments online.
You can also use PayPal system without a PayPal account, as a normal credit card payment system.

PayPal is a worlwide solution based on a small fee that must be paid for each donation received.
In this way there are no costs for the donor.
